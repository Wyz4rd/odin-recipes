Seafood chowder

Seafood chowder is a thick, creamy soup or stew made with a variety of seafood like fish, shrimp, and scallops, simmered in a dairy-based broth that can include milk or cream. It typically contains vegetables such as potatoes and onions, and is often seasoned with herbs and sometimes bacon or white wine. The ingredients are cooked to form a rich, hearty, and flavorful dish,often served with crusty bread for dipping.

ingredients

  • 1 medium carrot, finely chopped
  • 3 (750g) potatoes, peeled, roughly chopped
  • 1 stick celery, finely chopped
  • 4 cups (1litre) chicken stock
  • 2 corn cobs
  • 500g marinara mix
  • 200ml thickened cream
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh chives
  • White Sourdough Vienna, to serve
