Bangers and mash with red onion gravy

Bangers and mash is a traditional British dish consisting of sausages (bangers) served with mashed potatoes (mash) and a rich onion gravy. It is a popular and classic comfort food, often found on pub menus. The name "bangers" comes from British slang for sausages, which historically would "bang" or burst during cooking due to their high water content.

ingredients

  • 30g butter, melted
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 8 thick pork sausages
  • 1 large red onion, cut into thick wedges
  • 1/4 cup salt-reduced traditional gravy powder
  • 1/3 cup red wine
  • 1 cup Massel beef stock
  • 2 tbsp chopped fresh flat-leaf parsley
